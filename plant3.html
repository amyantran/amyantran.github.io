wheeler sotol, the closeted

A slow-growing fella, the Wheeler Sotol might remind you of the average closeted lesbian.

It takes up to ten years for the Wheeler Sotol to flower into its full and authentic self! That’s a long time for self-discovery--but it’s worth the wait. When it’s entirely grown, it is big, bold, and beautiful. Durable and drought-resistant, the Wheeler Sotol is resilient, even if it takes a while to get there.
